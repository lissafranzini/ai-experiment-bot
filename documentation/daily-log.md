# Daily Learning Log

Track your progress, insights, and challenges throughout the 30-day journey.

## How to Use This Log

- **Each day**: Copy the template below and fill it out
- **Be honest**: Record both successes and struggles  
- **Keep it brief**: 2-3 sentences per section is enough
- **Review weekly**: Look back to see your progress

---

## Daily Entry Template

```markdown
## Day [X] - [Date] - [Topic]

### Today's Focus
- [What you planned to learn/do]

### What I Learned
- [Key concepts, insights, or discoveries]

### What I Built/Practiced
- [Code written, projects worked on, exercises completed]

### Challenges
- [What was difficult, confusing, or frustrating]

### Wins
- [What worked well, breakthroughs, "aha" moments]

### Tomorrow's Goal
- [What you want to focus on next]

### Resources Used
- [Links, tutorials, documentation referenced]

### Time Spent: [X] minutes
```
